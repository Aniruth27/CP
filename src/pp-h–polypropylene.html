PP-H – POLYPROPYLENE (HOMOPOLYMER)

Product Description

PP-H - Polypropylene (homopolymer). Polypropylene homopolymer materials are extremely strong and highly resistant to chemicals, corrosion and heat. Chennai Polyplast offers a comprehensive range of PP-H products designed for high-performance industrial applications.

Type of product:

Compression moulded sheets, extruded sheets, round rods and welding rods

Colours:

Natural, grey, white, white 9010, special colour

Properties

  • • High rigidity
  • • Very high heat resistance
  • • Excellent weldability properties
  • • High chemical & corrosion resistance

Specification

  • • Extruded Sheets: 1mm to 50mm thickness
  • • Standard Sizes: 2000 x 1000 mm, 3000 x 1500 mm
  • • Other Sizes: 2440 x 1220 mm, 4000 x 2000 mm
  • • Rods: Available in Various diameters

Typical areas of application

  • Electroplating technology
  • Ventilation & equipment manufacturing
  • Laboratory technology
  • Pump engineering
