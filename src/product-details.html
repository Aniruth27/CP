HDPE – POLYETHYLENE SHEETS & RODS

Product Description

High Density Polyethylene Roc (HDPE) is a FDA Approved material of the highest quality with excellent impact resistance. HDPE has high tensile strength, low moisture absorption and is chemical and corrosion resistant. It is a light weight material that is non-toxic and non-staining and used in a variety of applications and industries.

Sizes Available

  • • Thickness available from: 1mm to 200m
  • • Sizes available: 1mt x 2mt. 122m x 2.44mt. 1.2mt x 2mt, 1.5mt x 3mt
  • • Rods Dia available from: 16mm dia to 400mm dia
  • • Squares available from 16mm sq to 150mm sq
  • • Profiles available as per customer requirement
